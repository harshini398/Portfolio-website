🍝 Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Grated cheese (for topping)
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil water with a pinch of salt. Cook spaghetti for 8–10 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil. Add onion and garlic, cook until soft.
  3. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper, oregano, and sugar. Simmer 10–15 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the sauce until well coated.
  5. Serve hot, topped with cheese and basil.
